JawSync Module

Synchronize jaw motion perfectly with audio playback for lifelike speech.

The JawSync Module brings animatronic speech to life by automatically syncing servo-driven jaw motion with recorded or live audio. Ideal for character heads, skulls, and puppets, it provides a plug-and-play experience with minimal configuration.

  • Automatic jaw movement synced to voice amplitude
  • Adjustable sensitivity and servo range controls
  • Plug-and-play design for quick setup
  • Compatible with Arduino and standard servo drivers
