ServoBuddy

Compact servo controller designed for animatronic projects.

The ServoBuddy is a versatile servo controller that simplifies motion control for animatronic projects. Whether you're designing moving eyes, jaws, or limbs, this compact unit ensures smooth, synchronized movements with minimal setup.

  • Controls up to 16 servos simultaneously
  • USB or Bluetooth programming interface
  • Compact 3D-printable housing included
  • Compatible with Arduino and Raspberry Pi
